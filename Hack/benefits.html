Benefits

  • Benefits of generic medicine
  • Generic medicine can be 90% less than the brand medicine.
  • It have same quality , which is safe and same power and quality as a brand medicine.
  • Generic makers don't face the same cost as manufacturers of brand-name drugs so they sell this medicine as less cost.
  • Since generics use the same active ingredients and are shown to work the same way in the body, they have the same risks and benefits as their brand-name counterparts.
  • Generic medicine is use to better the heart and it is bioequivalency.
  • generic drugs can be very cheap , which makes treatment affordable ,especially in developing country.
  • No precedural delay for production and distribution.
